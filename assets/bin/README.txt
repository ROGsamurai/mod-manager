7-Zip Console Binaries for RAR Support
=======================================

Download from: https://7-zip.org/download.html
Get: "7-Zip Extra: standalone console version" (7z2409-extra.7z or similar)

Extract and copy these 2 files into this folder:
  - 7z.exe
  - 7z.dll

That's it. The app will find them automatically.
